## The Product

BassForecast generates a **Bass Forecast Rating (BFR)** on a 1-10 scale for any North American location over a 10-day window.

### How BFR Works
- Real-time AccuWeather data (official partnership announced March 2024)
- Solunar calendars (moon phase feeding periods)
- Temperature transfer reports from water engineers
- Bass feeding behavior research from fishery biologists
- Tracks 9 seasons and 22 bait types
- Outputs: top 5 bait recommendations, peak feeding times, ideal water cover types per day
- Claims: 305% catch rate increase on "Epic" rated days, 68% on "Good" days vs study average

### Platforms
- iOS (App Store) — published by BassForecast, LLC
- Android (Google Play) — published by BassForecast

## Bait Advisor — B2B SaaS

Embeddable widget for tackle retailers:
- Analyzes 11,000+ data points (weather, water conditions, bass behavior)
- Provides top 5 bait recommendations for any location
- Retailers control which products appear in recommendations
- "No-risk, easy-to-embed"
- Boosts Average Order Value (AOV) through data-driven recommendations
- Provides customer behavior insights to retailers

**Verified impact:** Influenced **$1.8M in gross merchandising value (GMV)** for online tackle retailers (The Fishing Wire, June 2025).

**Live integration:** Googan Squad embeds it at googansquad.com/pages/bait-recommendations-powered-by-bassforecast

**Presented at ICAST 2025** (world's largest sportfishing trade show) as B2B offering.
