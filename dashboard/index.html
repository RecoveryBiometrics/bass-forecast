What's wrong exactly?

Wrong: tacklewarehouse.com/product.html?=BFOR (no parameter name)
Correct: tacklewarehouse.com/product.html?from=BFOR (has "from" key)

Both appear on the same pages — meaning someone got it right sometimes and wrong other times. Some links also have double errors like ?=BFOR?=BFOR or ?color=RCB?=BFOR (double question mark).
